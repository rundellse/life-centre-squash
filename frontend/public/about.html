The Plymouth Life Centre Squash League is an open squash league based in the Plymouth Life Centre. The league is open to all ages and abilities. If you would like to join us please feel free to contact us, or sign up with your details on the sheet which can be found on the board in the Life Centre squash courts.

Matches in the league are played according to England Squash rules, best of 5. Games are played to 15 points.
